## 1. Services Offered

Consultant provides the following professional services related to Aegis-DB deployment, optimization, and support:

- **Installation & Deployment** - Complete setup of Aegis-DB environments including cluster configuration, security hardening, and initial optimization
- **Migration Services** - Data migration from existing database systems to Aegis-DB with validation and testing
- **Performance Optimization** - Query tuning, index optimization, resource allocation, and throughput improvements
- **Architecture Review** - Assessment of current or proposed database architecture with recommendations
- **Compliance Audit Assistance** - Configuration and documentation support for regulatory compliance requirements
- **Custom Development** - Development of custom integrations, extensions, or tooling
- **Training & Workshops** - Technical training for development and operations teams

## 3. Terms and Conditions

### 3.1 Scheduling

- **Standard Engagements**: Require 1-2 week lead time for scheduling
- **Retainer Clients**: 48-hour scheduling for non-expedited requests
- **Expedited Engagements**: Work begins within 24-48 hours of agreement execution

### 3.2 Travel and On-Site Work

- **Remote Work**: All remote consulting is included in stated rates
- **On-Site Work**: Travel time and expenses billed at cost
- **On-Site Day Rate**: $2,000 per day plus reasonable travel expenses
- **Expense Policy**: Expenses require advance approval for amounts exceeding $500

### 3.3 Intellectual Property

- **Pre-Existing IP**: All intellectual property owned by either party prior to this engagement remains the sole property of that party
- **Custom Work Product**: Custom work product developed specifically for Client under this Agreement shall become Client's property upon full payment
- **Generic Improvements**: Consultant reserves the right to incorporate generic improvements, techniques, and knowledge gained during the engagement into Consultant's products and services, provided such incorporation does not include Client's confidential information

### 3.4 Confidentiality

Each party agrees to hold in confidence all confidential information disclosed by the other party during the term of this Agreement. Confidential information includes, but is not limited to, technical data, trade secrets, business plans, customer information, and proprietary methodologies. This obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

### 3.5 Limitation of Liability

IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, REGARDLESS OF THE CAUSE OF ACTION OR WHETHER SUCH PARTY HAS BEEN ADVISED OF THE POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES. CONSULTANT'S TOTAL LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE FEES PAID BY CLIENT IN THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

### 3.6 Payment Terms

- **Payment Due**: Net 30 days from invoice date
- **Late Payment Fees**: 1.5% per month on overdue balances
- **Accepted Methods**: Wire transfer, ACH, or credit card (3% processing fee for credit card)
- **Disputed Invoices**: Client must notify Consultant of any disputed charges within 15 days of invoice date
